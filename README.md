# 2022-2023-Football-Transfer-Market-Analysis
----
----

**Overview**
This dashboard provides a comprehensive financial and demographic analysis of the 2022-2023 Football Summer Transfer Window. Leveraging data visualization principles in Tableau, the project offers a quick, high-level snapshot of market activity, player valuation, and talent flow.

---
**Key Insights & Metrics**
The analysis is driven by key metrics and visualizations designed to answer critical questions about transfer market economics:

**Financial Volume:** Total transfers (2,000) and the ratio of paid vs. free/loan transfers (53.90%).

**Talent Valuation:** The "Top 10 Most Valued Signings" bar chart identifies players with the highest recorded market values.

**Market Focus:** The "Key Transfer Positions" pie chart shows the percentage distribution of transfers by player role, highlighting the most sought-after positions in the window.

**Player Demographics:** The "No. of Players by Age Group" chart breaks down transfer volume by age, revealing the market's focus on youth development versus experienced talent (Average Age: 25).

**Geospatial Analysis:** The "Geographic Distribution of Transfers" map provides a location-based context for every transfer, showing where talent is originating and where it is being acquired.

----

**Skills Demonstrated**
**Data Analysis:** Creating custom measures like Paid Transfer Rate (using IF/THEN logic) and isolating valuable players.

**Geospatial Visualization:** Utilizing Tableau's built-in mapping capabilities to show global distribution.

**Dashboard Design:** Creating a clear, dark-themed dashboard with effective visual hierarchy and KPI cards for immediate insight.

**Interactivity:** Implementing dashboard actions (e.g., filtering all charts by a selected country or position).
